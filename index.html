What is BMI?

The BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measurement of a person's weight. It is a way to calculate your body fat percentage depending on your height and weight. It is applicable to both males and women of legal age. It is a screening technique for determining whether or not a person is at a healthy weight.

BMI detects weight categories that may cause health concerns, but it does not diagnose an individual's body fatness or health. The significance of BMI is that it can help you maintain a healthy weight for your heart. You might also begin to look after your health by eating a heart-healthy diet and increasing your physical exercise.
